//CUSTOMIZATION//

/Fog's Crosshair/
Turned off by default, go into hudlayout.res located in the /scripts/ folder and edit both the "visible" and "enabled" value to 1. The crosshair legend is included in resource/fonts/crosshairs.png

/Custom Colors/
You are able to customize a couple of colors of the HUD, as located in clientscheme.res in the resource folder. They are labelled under //Custom Colors.
